Welcome

Welcome to Colors of Strength—a safe space to explore healing through creativity. Whether you are new to Art Therapy or coming back to the center of your healing, we invite you to discover community, and vibrance for your life!

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate spaces that honor both vulnerability and transformation—where expression becomes a bridge to recovery, and peace is something we find, not just alone, but together.

“Art enables us to find ourselves and lose ourselves at the same time.”
Thomas Merton

Membership

Here, art becomes a safe space—a way to process, express, and reconnect. Rooted in a journey through healing and lived experience, this community is built on the belief that creative expression can calm the storm inside us and offer peace like still water. Whether you're picking up a brush, a pen, or your voice, you're not alone. Welcome to a community where healing begins with expression. Today's your day to heal.
